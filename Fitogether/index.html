More effective Together

Federal Institute of Technology (EPFL) developed a mobile app called HealthyTogether, which allows users to participate in daily physical exercises as a game with their friends. The researchers conducted a two-week experiment with the application. Results show that users significantly enhanced physical activities using HealthyTogether compared with when they exercised alone by up to 15% . Among the group settings, cooperation (21% increase) and hybrid (18% increase) outperformed competition (8% increase) .

Chen, Y., & Pu, P. (2014, April). HealthyTogether: exploring social incentives for mobile fitness applications. In Proceedings of the second international symposium of chinese chi (pp. 25-34). ACM.
